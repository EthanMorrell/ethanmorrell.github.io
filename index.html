About Me

My name is Ethan Morrell. I was born and raised in Hawaii. I am currently a sophomore at James Campbell High School. Some of my interests are programing and robotics.

I only started programming recently. In between my 8th and 9th grade year I competed in the 8th e-ICON World Content, where I met a few students who graduated from the AP Computer Science program at JCHS. Since then I have taught myself HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and some Python.

I've been doing robotics since I was in the 6th grade. I have created and piloted both aquatic and terrestrial robots. I have been to a total of three international robotics competitions. I am the president and co-founder of JCHS Robotics.

I also enjoy hanging out with my family and friends.
